Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a new property?

You can register a new property by navigating to the "Add Property" section in your dashboard.

How do I contact customer support?

Customer support is available via the "Contact Us" page or by emailing us directly at support@propertyportal.com.

How can I update my property details?

To update property details, go to your dashboard, select the property, and click "Edit Details."
